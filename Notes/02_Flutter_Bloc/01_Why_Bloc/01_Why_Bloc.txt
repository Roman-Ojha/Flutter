*) Why Bloc?
    -> Business Logic Component
    -> Bloc is a Design Patter create by google to help separate business logic from the presentation layer and enable developer to reuse code more easily
    -> There is also the state management library which help developer to implement bloc design pattern in there flutter application

*) What are the Advantages?
    -> Understand easily what's happening inside you app
    -> More Structured code, Easier to maintain and test
    -> Know & Understand every state of you app
    -> Work on a Single, Stable, Popular and Effective Bloc CodeBase
